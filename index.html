About Us

About Urinalysis

At Urinalysis, our mission is to revolutionize healthcare by empowering individuals with quick, reliable, and accurate health diagnostics. Our platform leverages advanced technology to analyze urine strip tests, providing instant insights into your health. Whether you’re monitoring your wellness or identifying potential health concerns, we are here to make the process seamless and effective.

At Urinalysis, we firmly believe that early detection and regular health checks form the foundation of a healthier, more proactive lifestyle. Early detection plays a crucial role in identifying potential health issues before they escalate into serious conditions, giving individuals the opportunity to seek timely medical attention and take preventive measures.

How We Works ?

Image Upload

Enable users to upload an image of their urine strip for analysis and prepare it for processing.

Read More

Image Segmentation and Color Detection

Segment the urine strip from the background and detect the colors for each test area.Divide the strip into its individual test regions. These regions correspond to different chemical indicators on the strip.

Read More

Data Mapping and Analysis

Map the detected colors to predefined health indicators and analyze the results. Compare the extracted color values against a predefined database or model that correlates colors with specific conditions (e.g., glucose, ketones, pH levels).

Read More

Report Generation

Generate a clear, user-friendly report summarizing the analysis results. Based on the analysis, determine whether the user is healthy or has potential issues (e.g., dehydration, infection, or other conditions).
